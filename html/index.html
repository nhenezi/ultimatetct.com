What is Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe?

Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe is a 2 player board game similar to Tic-Tac-Toe. You are playing 10 Tic-Tac-Toe game simultaneously on one 9x9 board!

How do I win?

In order to win, you have to win three in a row on major board and to do that you have to win three in a row on minor board.

How do I win on minor boards?

Rules on minor boards are similar to standard Tic-Tac-Toe with one twist: When you play on a minor board, your opponent is forced to play on that major board. Take a look at the picture on left, you have placed O in top-right corner. So your oponnent is forced to play on top-right major board.

What if I force opponent on already won/tied field?

Then you oponnent has no restrictions on next move; i.e. he can play anywhere!
